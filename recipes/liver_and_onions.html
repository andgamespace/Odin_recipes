Palak Paneer, or Spinach Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 green chile peppers, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons chopped garlic
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground red chiles
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 8 ounces paneer, cubed

    • Directions

    Step 1:

    Heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil in a skillet over moderate heat, mixing it in the oil until it's soft. Move it to a blender, puree the spinach until it becomes a coarse paste.

    Step 2:

    Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium heat, cook cumin in hot oil until there is an aroma. Add onion, green chile peppers, and garlic. Cook and stir until onion is tender, 3-4 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and cover saucepan. Simmer for a minute, adding blended spinach paste, water, ground coriander, red chile powder, and salt. Cook until heated through, should take around 2.5 minutes.

    Step 3:

    Stir the paneer into the mixture, letting it simmer until it's hot.
