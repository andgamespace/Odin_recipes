Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and three quarters of a cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract

    • Directions

    Step 1:

    Preheat the waffle iron. Beat eggs in a large bowl using a hand beater until they're fluffy.

    Step 2:

    Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour the mix onto a hot waffle iron. Cook the waffles until they're golden brown. Best served warm.
